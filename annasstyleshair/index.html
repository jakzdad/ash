Color Care

Use a shampoo formulated for color treated hair

  • Sulfate free shampoos also work well on color treated hair.
  • Use a good conditioner because hydrated hair retains color better.
  • Try not to wash hair everyday. Over washing doesn’t just strip away natural oils, it can also slowly strip away hair color as well as leave your hair dry.

BLONDE, GRAY AND WHITE

  • It is recommended to use shampoo made specifically for blonde/gray/white hair.
  • This shampoo is a deep purple blue color which neutralizes brassy tones that hair picks up and brightens it.

VIVID AFTER CARE

  • Only wash your hair once or twice per week maximum, once a week is preferred.
  • When washing hair use tepid water.
  • To make the color last longer, when cleansing it is recommended to shampoo the root area, but not let the shampoo sit on the rest of the hair for any lengthy period.
  • Clip hair up and/or use a shower cap while showering on the days you are not washing your hair.
  • Use a dry shampoo in between washes.
  • Avoid getting hair wet while swimming. This includes pools, hot tubs, lakes, and the ocean.
  • Avoid sun exposure or use a big floppy hat. UV rays have been know to pull color out of hair.
  • Use salon quality products at home. Certain shampoos and conditioners are formulated to maintain vivids colors longer.
  • A deep conditioner, smoothing serum, and thermal protectant are also highly recommended.
  • You’ve spent a lot of time and money on your hair, you don’t want it to be washed down the drain by inferior products.
